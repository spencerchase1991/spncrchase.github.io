    About Me

    Hi, my name is Spencer! I was born in Fruita, Colorado, January 17, 1991. I have two Razor Edge Pitbulls that take up the whole bed. I would love to tell you that I take full advantage of living in a state full of adventure, but I do not! I have a huge family and love every minute of it. Minus Christmas shopping and remembering birthdays!! We are all very close and we love trying new food and drinks.

    Graduated in 2009 from Parker, Colorado and spent most of my early 20's serving tables and having fun! Right after my 24th birthday, I landed a receptionist job at The Society for Mining Metallurgy and Exploration. Quickly moved my way up to the IT department, where I worked under two Senior developers and became intrigued with their knowledge of code.

    My hopes are to see the world. I believe Coding and Web Development will allow me to do just that. I am excited to see where this career will take me in life!!
